## Purpose

The purpose of this project brief is to provide a clear framework for the
development of a Developer Onboarding System. It documents the business need,
target users, project goals, scope, deliverables, risks and success metrics to
ensure stakeholder alignment and informed decision-making throughout the
project. The resulting onboarding documentation will support intern and junior
developers in becoming productive more quickly within the fictional startup
company HealthTech.

---

## Business Problem

The fictional HealthTech startup lacks a centralized and standardized onboarding
system for intern and junior developers. Critical onboarding information is
distributed across multiple sources, documentation is inconsistent, and
onboarding expectations are not clearly communicated. Consequently, new
developers depend heavily on senior engineers for guidance, reducing the time
available for development work and slowing team productivity. The absence of a
structured onboarding experience also results in inconsistent knowledge
transfer, longer ramp-up times, and varying levels of readiness among new team
members.

---

## User Problem

New developers at the fictional HealthTech startup include interns, career
changers, and junior developers with varying levels of technical experience. As
they begin onboarding, they often struggle to understand technical terminology,
development workflows, and project structures. Currently, onboarding information
is difficult to navigate and assumes varying levels of prior knowledge. This
creates confusion, information overload, and uncertainty about how to complete
required onboarding tasks. As a result, new developers may lack confidence,
depend heavily on senior team members for guidance, and experience delays in
becoming productive contributors.

---

## Project Goals

### Goal 1

Create a centralized onboarding system that consolidates onboarding resources,
documentation, and setup instructions into a single, accessible location for new
developers.

### Goal 2

Reduce onboarding confusion and support requests by providing structured setup
guides, standardized development workflows, and troubleshooting resources that
enable developers to resolve common issues independently.

### Goal 3

Enable new developers to independently complete environment setup, access
required resources, and make their first code contribution within two business
days of beginning onboarding.

## Scope

### Included

- Welcome documentation
- Developer setup guide
- Quick start guide
- Git workflow documentation
- First task tutorial
- Troubleshooting guide
- Glossary
- Documentation sitemap
- Learning path curriculum
- Accessibility-focused design recommendations

### Excluded

- Actual software development
- Live production systems
- Internal company databases
- Employee HR onboarding
- Security compliance implementation
